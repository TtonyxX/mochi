What are Mochi Squares?

We make homemade mochi in a unique way, based in Los Altos, CA, and sell it on this website to those near us. It includes both mochi, ice cream, and other toppings. Ordering includes choosing these toppings and the quantity, and after buying, we deliver the mochi to your specified location.

How it's made

For the mochi, we use coconut milk, mochi flour, vanilla extract and sugar. We then add the ice cream and toppings, put it into a sandwich, and serve it.
